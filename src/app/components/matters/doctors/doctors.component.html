Can we migrate to
100% online? Not yet!

Physicians still prefer in-person contact, even after the pandemic. A great part of the content absorption and performance is due to the relationship with the representative/company. Interpersonal relationships are still very important and must be considered in a mixed format with new digital technologies.

In which event format should we invest?

In-person events vs. Online events

IN-PERSON EVENTS

For live classes/discussion groups/debates and immersion courses, in-person was chosen as the best format

ONLINE EVENTS

Most participants (81%) preferred to watch recorded classes/lectures online

ADVANTAGES OF IN-PERSON EVENTS:
Interaction between lecturer and audience, networking, get to know a new place and event organizer.

In-person format ensures relationship and discussion among professionals, which is highly valued for the purpose of obtaining updates.

ADVANTAGES OF ONLINE EVENTS:
Convenience, content available online, travel and finding a time in the calendar.
The ideal time for a digital event is on weekdays (Wednesday), in the evening, with average duration of 1 hour.

The online format is good for content history, subsequent consultation, and is a practical way of receiving content.
