In which event format should we invest?

In-person events vs. Online events

IN-PERSON EVENTS

In-person events are preferred (50%) for addressing content related to brands and products. As to general scientific matters or those related to their specialty, 40% prefer in-person meetings and other 40% prefer digital media

ONLINE EVENTS

Most participants (83%) preferred to watch recorded classes/lectures with presentations online; for other kinds of events (live classes/discussion groups/debates and immersion courses), in-person was chosen as the best format.

ADVANTAGES OF IN-PERSON EVENTS:
Access to printed materials and promotional gifts/networking/interaction between lecturer and audience.

ADVANTAGES OF ONLINE EVENTS:
Convenience/content available online/being able to adjust the congress program to daily routine -> The ideal time for a digital event is on weekends (Saturday), in the morning or evening, with average duration of 2 hours.
