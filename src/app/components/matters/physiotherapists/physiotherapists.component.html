Can we migrate to
100% online? Not yet!

Doctors still prefer in-person contact, even after the pandemic. A great part of the content absorption and performance is due to the relationship with the representative/company. Interpersonal relationships are still very important and must be considered in a mixed format with new digital technologies.

In which event format should we invest?

In-person events vs. Online events

IN-PERSON EVENTS

In-person is the most preferred format, both for content related to brands and products and for specialty-related general matters

ONLINE EVENTS

For events such as debates, discussion groups and immersion courses, the in-person format is the best option.

ADVANTAGES OF IN-PERSON EVENTS:
Interaction between lecturer and audience, access to printed materials and promotional gifts.

ADVANTAGES OF ONLINE EVENTS:
Convenience and content available online. The ideal time for a digital event is on Saturday mornings, with average duration of 2 hours.
Online events are better accepted by professionals from the Southeast. Again, there is an opportunity for a mixed approach, focusing on networking and access to materials in in-person events, and more dense content, which can be accessed later, in online events.
