In which event format should we invest?

In-person events vs. Online events

IN-PERSON EVENTS



For events such as debates, discussion groups and immersion courses, the in-person format is the best option.

ONLINE EVENTS



Only for receiving content from recorded or live classes/lectures, the online format is the most preferred.

ADVANTAGES OF IN-PERSON EVENTS:
Closer contact, interaction, convenience, and available content.

ADVANTAGES OF ONLINE EVENTS:
Convenience, content available online and schedule adjustments. The ideal time for a digital event is on Saturday evenings, with average duration of 2 hours.
