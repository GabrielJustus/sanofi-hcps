Physical therapists are the ones who are least contacted by the pharmaceutical industry

The percentage of those who are not visited is also high among nutritionists (40%), pharmacists (51%) and clerks (58%).

WHAT THEY WOULD LIKE TO GET FROM PHARMACEUTICAL COMPANIES: Educational materials (doctors, nutritionists, physical therapists, and pharmacists) and applications for patients (clerks).

WHAT THEY WOULD LIKE TO SEE ON A DIGITAL PLATFORM: Scientific content (doctors, nutritionists, physical therapists, and pharmacists) and information on products (clerks).

The main interest in being contacted by the pharmaceutical industry for all categories is receiving educational content and samples.


Doctors, pharmacists, and clerks prefer in-person visits, while nutritionists and physical therapists prefer visits by digital means (e.g.: video call).


FREQUENCY OF VISITS from the representative is the most crucial factor to make a company stand out from the others, for all categories.
