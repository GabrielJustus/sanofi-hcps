Access to printed materials and promotional gifts/networking/interaction between lecturer and audience

ONLINE EVENTS

Convenience/content available online/being able to adjust the congress program to daily routine

- For other types of events (live classes/discussion groups/debates and immersion courses), the in-person mode was chosen as the best option by doctors, nutritionists, and physical therapists.

- Recorded classes/lectures with slides were preferred by all professionals in the online mode;

- Drugstore workers (pharmacists and clerks) prefer the online mode the most in comparison with the other professionals.

For live classes/ lectures with slides, nutritionists are divided between in- person (50%) and online (50%) modes.
